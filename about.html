Method

How I approach analysis

Ask the right questions

Before I touch the data I sit with the problem. What decision are you actually trying to make, and what would a genuinely useful answer look like? Getting this right means I solve your real problem and not my guess at it.

Explore the data

Then I get to know the data honestly and look at distributions, outliers, gaps and the odd things that don't add up. I let the data argue with my assumptions before I commit to any story.

Clean & prepare

This is where I fix whatever exploration turned up, from duplicates and mixed rating scales to broken imports and even fabricated rows. I shape the data until it's genuinely ready to analyse and visualise.

Visualise & advise

Finally I build the dashboard around your decision, and I never stop at the charts. Every project ends with plain-language business advice you can act on the same day.
